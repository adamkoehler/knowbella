Executive Summary

What if we told you that we can both reward scientists for doing the science that they love and incentivize them for collaboration and peer review? What if we told you that we reduce the time to publication through a preprint server? What if we said that we could provide underserved scientists with IP that would previously have languished without worth and costing money.

Our Audience

Scientists. They are the most highly educated population on Earth. They increase the knowledge that changes the way we understand our world and the universe. Scientists are influencers and decision-makers at every level. Among the most highly regarded are Nobel Laureates. Scientists are influencers and key opinion leaders in their professional societies, communities, and governments around the world.

Our Token

With the Helix™ token as the “The Currency of Science™”, we will move the Knowbella Platform™, from beta to public launch. Helix™ will be awarded to scientists who collaborate within the platform. Through our efforts, scientists will be able to transact their Helix™ with vendors and service providers. This forms an economic system for the scientific platform where scientists can spend their Helix™ for goods and services and exchange Helix™ with each other.

The Problem

Orphan intellectual property

Unused IP sits on the shelves of many corporations, universities, inventor workspaces and other locations for reasons such as budgetary constraints, lack of market insights, lack of awareness of the IP, or lack of perceived marketability and value. Technology Transfer Officers must make tough decisions on which IP has marketability. However, they often make those decisions based on the limits of the jurisdiction in which the IP was filed. When that IP is put in front of the global scientific community, many different opportunities never previously envisioned may be realized.

Our Solution

The Knowbella Platform™, through its end-to-end scientific collaboration ecosystem, is designed to provide an area where scientists can freely collaborate. This is particularly important for underserved scientists around the world.

The platform will unlock orphaned IP and provide significant rewards and incentives to researchers to advance science and global collaboration. Knowbella will not only host orphan, dormant, and open IP, but it will provide grants and free laboratory equipment. Scientists can also have access to manuscript development software, preprint server, and cryptocurrency tokens for using the platform.

The Mission

Knowbella Tech’s mission is to break down the silos of knowledge created by patents that currently exist in some areas of the world. This openness of research and development provides a social good by generating economic incentives while improving the world.

The Knowbella Platform™ potentially brings value to the nearly $4T of unused IP.

One can view Knowbella Tech as the matching service for orphan/dormant IP and IP consumers. Most IP consumers within the Knowbella Platform™ are scientists and researchers.

Breaks down silos of information and know-how, enabling the sharing with other scientists;

Matches IP providers and IP consumers with similar interests – an opportunity that simply does not exist in a science and IP space that has labored under a “protectionist” mentality;

Provides economic rewards (Helix™) and decentralized management of grant funding for research. A number of other blockchain enabled features will be developed. This includes tracking the provenance of IP, decentralized identity, and managing research reproducibility.

Gives every scientific improvement or contribution based on that open IP back to the community via a CC 4.0 and copyleft open licensing model.. This makes each advance more valuable for the next researcher that looks at it, adopts it, and takes it further, and so forth. One might think of this as open source for science. We call it "open science."

Knowbella Tech’s objectives are to generate triple bottom line returns for our stakeholders, thaWe will help scientists and Helix™ holders around the world improve their research, disseminate that research more broadly, and increase the speed of publications; all the while getting paid to do it.
